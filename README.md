# **MindTech AI – Mental Wellness Assistant**

## **Description**
MindTech is an AI-powered mental wellness platform designed to provide safe, accessible, and personalized emotional support. It addresses the growing issue of stress and loneliness by offering a confidential space where users can interact with an empathetic AI chatbot, track their mood over time, access therapeutic tools, and connect with certified counselors when needed. MindTech is for anyone seeking emotional support—whether they are experiencing daily stress, loneliness, or simply want to maintain their mental well-being.

## **Features**
- **AI-Powered Emotional Chatbot** – Detects user mood from text and offers real-time empathetic responses.
- **Mood Tracking & Visualization** – Log daily emotions and view progress through interactive charts and insights.
- **Wellness Toolkit** – Access guided exercises, journaling prompts, and self-reflection activities.
- **Counselor Booking System** – Seamlessly schedule appointments with mental health professionals via Google Calendar integration.
- **Personalized Wellness Recommendations** – AI suggests exercises and resources based on mood history.
